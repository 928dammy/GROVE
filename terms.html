Deed of Zeal

The Givers' Covenant

1. The Irrevocable Seed

By planting your gift, you acknowledge that the principal amount is **consumed to fuel the hunt.** It is no longer a withdrawable balance. Like a seed in the ground, it cannot be recovered; it can only be grown.

2. The Justice Harvest

The House commits to tending your seed until your **Justice Harvest** reaches its exhaustion point (2.0x). Rewards are streamed based on the success of informal deals and gadget flips.

3. The Gardener's Discretion

The timing and frequency of reward "drops" are managed by the House. Seniority and "Justice Weight" are used to ensure the community is rewarded fairly during high-profit hunts.

4. The Social Halo

A portion of every successful hunt is dedicated to environmental recovery and social study lamps. Your gift is not just for you; it is for the restoration of the land.

"We do not bank. We hunt. We do not invest. We plant."
